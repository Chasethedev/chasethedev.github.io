A little more about us

Our award winning pizza have people coming back and asking for more. We're home to the award winning pizza's Smoking Goodfella's and the Chicken Garlic Blanco. We have a large dining area with large screen TV's to watch your game highlights of choice. We offer over 15 types of refreshments and an assortment of appetizers. We have a great setting for family and friends and free delivery if you choose to order take out!

  • Halal friendly alternatives and options available
  • Homemade recipes, we purchase produce from local farmer markets
  • Great place for family as we have an expanded menu to satisfy all guests from salads to burgers and calzones
  • Dine-in, takeout or free delivery
